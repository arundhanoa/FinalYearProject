Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I register for an event?

You can register for an event by visiting the event page and clicking on the "Sign Up" button.

Your registered events can be viewed on the "My Events" page, accessible from the main navigation menu.

3. What should I do if I encounter issues?

If you encounter any issues, please contact our support team via the contact form on the "Contact Us" page.
